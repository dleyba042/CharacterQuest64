Welcome...

This app will allow users to create a custom character to play through an adventure scenario. The character stats are based on a random roll of ranges. Stats would include strength, intellect, stamina, dexterity, and luck. Character customization would include fantasy races (i.e. elves, orcs, dwarves, humans). Races will dictate the ranges of stats possible for each character. For example, an Orc would have a higher range of strength and Elves would have a higher range of intellect. The app will have a basic inventory system. Different paths can open up depending on what the character is carrying. The inventory will have basic items like a key(to open paths), potions(to boost stats), and money.
